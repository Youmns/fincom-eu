Financial Commission is one of the most trusted dispute resolution bodies for Forex and Blockchain that has been protecting traders since 2013.

We unite more than 40 trusted and verified brokers, exchanges and technology providers who are committed to upholding the highest standards of commercial honor and best business practices in their operations.

Bring additional value and trust to their services

Increase customer confidence with fairness and transparency

Protect clients’ interests with access to free dispute resolution

By joining the Financial Commission brokers and exchanges:

Ensure quality trade executions for all customers

Resolve complaints in a quick, unbiased and authentic manner

Mitigate reputational risks in a stress-free and cost-effective way
